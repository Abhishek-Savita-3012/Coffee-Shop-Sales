# Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard** is an Excel-based data analytics project created to analyze retail sales data and generate actionable business insights for improving coffee shop performance.

The main objective of this project is to analyze retail sales data and gain useful insights that can help enhance the performance of the coffee shop.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this project is to understand coffee shop sales performance using an interactive dashboard and identify important business patterns such as:

- Peak sales hours
- Best-performing days
- Store-wise sales performance
- Top-selling products
- Product category contribution
- Customer footfall trends
- Average bill and order per person

---

## 📌 Key Insights

### 1. Sales by Day of the Week

Sales/footfall are highest on:

- Friday: 21,701
- Monday: 21,643
- Thursday: 21,654

The lowest footfall is on:

- Saturday: 20,510

This shows that weekdays perform better compared to Saturday.

---

### 2. Sales by Hour of the Day

The dashboard shows that sales activity increases strongly during morning hours.

Peak sales time is:

- 8 AM to 10 AM

The highest quantity ordered is around:

- 10 AM

This indicates that morning is the most important time period for the coffee shop.

---

### 3. Total Sales Revenue

The total sales revenue shown in the dashboard is:

**$698,812.33**

The dashboard includes month filters from January to June, so this total represents the selected period shown in the dashboard.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase staff availability during peak morning hours, especially from 8 AM to 10 AM.
- Keep high-selling products like Barista Espresso, Brewed Chai Tea, and Hot Chocolate well-stocked.
- Run offers or promotions on Saturday to improve low footfall.
- Promote Coffee and Tea products as they contribute the highest sales share.
- Create combo offers with Bakery items to increase average bill value.
- Use Hell's Kitchen store performance as a benchmark for other locations.
- Focus on inventory planning based on high-demand products and peak sales hours.

## 🏁 Conclusion

This project successfully analyzes coffee shop sales data using Microsoft Excel and presents the results through an interactive dashboard.

The analysis shows that the coffee shop performs best during morning hours, especially between 8 AM and 10 AM. Coffee and Tea are the most important product categories, and Hell's Kitchen is the strongest store location.

These insights can help the business improve staffing, inventory planning, promotions, and overall sales strategy.
